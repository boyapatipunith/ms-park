Squamates are a monophyletic sister group to the rhynchocephalians, members of the order Rhynchocephalia. ... Scientists believe crown group squamates probably originated in the Early Jurassic based on the fossil record. The first fossils of geckos, skinks and snakes appear in the Middle Jurassic.
